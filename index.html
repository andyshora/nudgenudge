nugenudge

nudge your users towards your goals with annoying arrows

Notes

  • Nudge length is 30-50px horizontally, regardless of arrow size. Sowwy, I haven't been arsed to create dynamic keyframe animations yet, but feel free to edit the CSS!

Supported Browsers

  • Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox, probably Safari, probably IE10 if they've got their shit together.
